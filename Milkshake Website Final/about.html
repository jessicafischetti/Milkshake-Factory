About

The Milkshake Factory is a family owned dessert resturant that is famous for its milkshakes and optional rooftop setting. The milkshake facotry has been a success since 1995 when its first open. Come in and enjoy a milk shake or a variety of different desserts with friends and family. We also entertain with live music on Thursday,Friday and Saturdays from 5pm to 12pm.
